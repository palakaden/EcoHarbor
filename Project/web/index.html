    About Us

    Our Waste Management System simplifies and manages all waste collection online. Designed to address the rising waste management issues in rural areas like Avoly, Ernakulam district, it streamlines waste collection activities.

    Our Mission

    Our mission is to implement a systematic waste collection approach, ensuring efficient and proper disposal to reduce environmental pollution and promote a cleaner living environment.

    Our Plan

    The Panchayath is actively collecting waste and plans to launch an online platform for scheduling and payment. This initiative seeks to reduce pollution and promote responsible waste disposal.

    Our Vision

    Entails efficiently collecting and disposing of waste, while promoting responsible waste management practices among residents. The goal is to set a positive example for other rural areas with similar challenges.

    Services

    Welcome to EcoHarbor Services! Our initiatives are geared towards creating a cleaner, greener community. Explore our range of services below and join us in building a more sustainable future for Avoly.

    Online Waste Collection Scheduling

    Residents can conveniently schedule waste pickups online through a user-friendly interface

    Waste Segregation Guidelines and Resources

    Community Education Workshops

    Learn about responsible waste management through interactive workshops and seminars

    Online Payment and Billing

    Make waste management payments conveniently online

    Community Feedback and Suggestions

    Share feedback and suggestions to improve the waste management system

    Collaboration with Environmental Agencies

    Partnering with environmental agencies to enhance waste management efforts
